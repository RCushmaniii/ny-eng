## 7 Conversation Starters

**Quick Challenge:** Think of your last awkward networking moment. What stopped you? Jot down one goal for your next chat. Ready? Let’s dive in.

### 1. Comment on the Moment

> “Phew, the Wi‑Fi’s cooperating today—miracle!”

A light observation builds instant rapport.  
**Follow‑up:** “Where are you joining from today—home base or somewhere new?”  
**Pro Tip:** In person? Try: “This venue’s sleek—have you been here before?”

### 2. Share a Light Win

> “Just tried the café downstairs—game‑changing espresso.”

Mini‑reviews spark curiosity.  
**Follow‑up:** “Found any hidden gems near the office?”

### 🤝 Dos & Don’ts of Friendly Small‑Talk

| ✅ **Do**                                                                  | 🚫 **Don’t**                                                |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| 😄 _Smile_ and keep an **open posture** (arms uncrossed)                   | Bring up **politics** or 💰 **salary**                      |
| 👂 _Listen actively_ — nod and ask follow‑ups like “_What happened next?_” | Share **overly personal** or sensitive stories              |
| ❓ Ask **open‑ended questions** such as “_What’s your take on …?_”         | **Dominate the conversation** — aim for a **50/50** balance |
